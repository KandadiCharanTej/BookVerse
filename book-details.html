The Midnight Library

by Matt Haig

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 4.8 out of 5 (2,485 reviews)
Author: Matt Haig
Published: August 13, 2020
Pages: 288
Language: English

Nora Seed finds herself in the Midnight Library, a bewildering place between life and death. With the help of an old librarian, she is given the chance to undo her regrets and see the consequences of the choices she didn't make. This is a novel about all of the choices that go into a life well lived.

With a voice both intimate and sweeping, Haig explores the profound human desire to have another chance at life, to undo past mistakes, and to know what might have been. It is a meditation on the meaningfulness of our choices, and what it means to truly live a full, purposeful life.
